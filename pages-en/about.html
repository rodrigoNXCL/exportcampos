About Us

Learn more about our history, mission and vision

Our History

ExportCampos was born in 1999 as a partnership dedicated to exporting frozen fruits and vegetables. From the very beginning we have been committed to quality and excellence at every step of the process.

We started with small processing batches in Linares, Chile, and over time we have grown to become a benchmark for high-quality frozen product exports to international markets.

Mission

Our mission is to connect the best frozen products from Chile with global markets, guaranteeing the highest quality in every shipment and maintaining long-lasting business relationships with our customers.

Vision

To be recognized as leaders in the frozen food export sector, standing out for our innovation, sustainable practices and contribution to the development of local communities.

Our Values

Quality

Committed to the highest quality standards in all our processes and products.

Sustainability

We implement environmentally responsible practices and support the communities where we operate.

Teamwork

We value collaboration and joint effort to achieve our goals.
