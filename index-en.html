About Us

We are a family-owned company dedicated to exporting frozen fruits and vegetables, operating since 1999 with the vision of bringing the flavors of Chile to the world. Our story is one of passion for processes and commitment to quality.

At ExportCampos, our mission is to connect our products with global markets, ensuring excellence at every step. Our vision is to be leaders in the industry, recognized for our innovation and sustainable practices, contributing to the development of the communities around us.
